# FAQ

## Is this a workflow engine?

No. Motus Work Ledger is workflow-neutral. Workflow tools do the work. Motus
records durable work facts and receipts.

## Is this employee surveillance?

No. Motus records consequential work facts, not worker activity streams.

## Is this a compliance product?

No. Work Receipts can support review and audit evidence, but Motus does not make
an organization compliant.

## Is this a blockchain?

No. Motus Work Ledger is local-first and does not require a chain, token, or
global authority ledger.

## Do I need `/motus`?

No. `/motus` is the reference implementation. Other systems can emit
Motus-compatible Work Receipts by following this repo.
